Introducing Malaysia’s New Property Developments

Here at LBS Bina Group, we are on a mission to consistently deliver excellence through our exceptional performance and progressive creations, while creating value for all our stakeholders and enriching the lives of communities across Malaysia.

Read More Close

Top Property Developer in Malaysia

As one of Malaysia’s top developers, we have established a solid foundation in designing built environments of the highest quality.

With our steadfast dedication, we strive to continuously enhance the lives and overall well-being of the communities we serve.

As a leading property and housing developer, LBS Bina Group is also committed to developing projects that promote sustainable living, as well as working and recreational environments for all.

Enhancing lifestyles with new development houses for sale

As mentioned, LBS Bina Group strives not only to build high-quality homes but also to create environments where families can enjoy a balanced and enriching lifestyle within their neighborhood

To properly meet the needs of thriving communities, our current property developments are built in strategic locations with improved connectivity such as access to major highways.

Apart from that, our housing development projects offer many security features and conveniences that are essential to meet the needs of residents for that much-needed peace of mind.

Furthermore, communities will also be able to revel in the comforts and enjoy surrounding facilities designed to improve their quality of life.

LBS Bina Group also focuses on providing affordable housing solutions to meet the growing demand for affordable and quality homes. With our innovative building technologies, materials and techniques, LBS aims to offer homes that cater to the needs of all families and individuals.

Affordable Yet Luxurious Housing Property

“Affordable luxury” is now within your grasp with our residential housing development projects.

As the demand for more affordable homes increases in the country, we are making high-quality homes, whether in the form of high-rise condos or landed properties, more accessible to all Malaysians.

Being property developers in Malaysia, we understand that high-quality residences are not only essential for enhancing the quality of life in communities but also to inspire them.

Besides offering one of the best new housing developments, LBS Bina Group is known for developing commercial projects to provide better infrastructure for surrounding communities to thrive.

We believe that this can help to contribute to the growth of the communities as well as enhance their quality of life.

Best housing & property developer that let you explore new and enriching projects

In addition, LBS Bina has expanded into property development projects in the hospitality and tourism sector by opening SCAPES Hotel Genting Highlands, marking a watershed moment in the company’s history.

As a property developer company in Malaysia, this is also the beginning of a gradual yet ever-evolving growth of LBS Bina property from building living spaces to creating experiences that further enrich lives.

Another one of LBS Bina Group’s involvement in tourism is the Zhuhai International Circuit, which is an overseas business investment in China that has hosted several international racing competitions.

Offering More Than Just Property Development Projects

LBS Bina promises to deliver property development projects that are of the highest quality in the housing sector but also in the commercial sector.

This is done by providing businesses with the choice of owning or leasing land, which we believe can help increase the opportunities for enterprises to prosper and develop.

Our M3 Shopping Mall is a perfect example of a commercial property development project that provides businesses with a chance to grow.

M3 Shopping Mall is strategically located in the vibrant township of Taman Melati, which is conveniently within reach and aspires to offer the surrounding communities value and opportunities through a range of daily conveniences.

Some of the popular and convenient retails located in M3 shopping mall are Mr D.I.Y., Tekstil Nagoya, 2CO Eco shop and The Store (Supermarket & Department Store).
